Odin Recipes

Traditionally made with cocoyam wrapped with cocoyam leaves

Ingredients

  • cocoyam
  • crayfish
  • palmoil
  • onion
  • ginger,gallic,pepper,maggi,salt

Steps

  1. cut cocoyam leaves to desired sizes
  2. peel off cocoyams and wash
  3. grate cocoyams
  4. wrap the mixture with leaves into desired sizes
  5. add ingredients
  6. serve on a plate and enjoy your meal
